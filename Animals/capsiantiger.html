NAME: Caspian Tiger (Panthera tigris virgata)

LENGTH: Approximately 9 - 10 feet (2.7M-3M)

HEIGHT: (at shoulder) around 3.3 feet (1M)

WEIGHT: 200-240 kg (440-530 lb)

KINGDOM(CLASSIFICATION): Animalia

DIET: Carnivore

HABITAT:(PLACE OF RESIDING) Historically found in the forests and grasslands of the Caspian Sea region

AVERAGE LIFESPAN: About 8-10 years in the wild

STATUS: Extinct

REASON OF EXTINCTION:Habitat loss and hunting

RIVALRY: No natural rivals
